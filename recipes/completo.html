Completo

Description:

this is the famous Completo Italiano

this is the best sandwich in the world

Ingredient:

  • 1 hot dog bread
  • 1 Sausage
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Avocado

Steps:

  1. toast the bread until crispy
  2. cooking the saudage for 10 minute
  3. chop the tomato into cube a
  4. grind the avocado and add salt to taste
  5. Assemble the whole thing in the following way: inside the bread, add the sausage, then the tomato, and finally the avocado.
  6. additionally you can add to your complete any sauge you like, example mayonnaise
