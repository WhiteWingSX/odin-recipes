banana Milk

Description:

this is a banana milk

a good option for the hot days

Ingredient:

  • 2 banana
  • 1L milk
  • 20g honey
  • 15g cocoa powder

Steps:

  1. In a blender add the two peeled bananas along with the milk, honey and cocoa, proceed to blend for approximately 1 minute.
  2. Additionally, you can add sugar to taste for greater sweetness.
