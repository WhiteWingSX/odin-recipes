Spaguetti with sauce

Description:

the classic spaguetti with sauce

the best option for begginer

Ingredient:

  • 1 noodles package
  • 500ml tomato sauce
  • salt
  • oil
  • 1 garlic

Steps:

  1. Cook the noodles in a pot of boiling water with salt to taste for 10 minutes.
  2. Strain them and add a little oil and a clove of ground garlic.
  3. Add the tomato sauce to the noodles and stir until well mixed.
